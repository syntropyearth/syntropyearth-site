Climate and healthcare strategy, scoped to your decision.

Every engagement answers the same question: where is climate already costing you, and what do you do about it? A named flagship, a recurring engagement, and a tight wider practice. Never a catalogue.

The flagship

CSR Strategy and Activation

Multi-year · cross-functional

Turn your CSR mandate into a multi-year activation that works across your functions, and earns you partnerships across your industry.

You already have the budget and the obligation. We design the strategy and the mid-to-long-term activation plan that makes the spend count: aligned across your internal functions, built to satisfy the obligation and stand up to the audit, and structured to bring valuable partnerships across industry rather than scattered, one-off activity.

The result is a programme that does more than report cleanly. It builds real health resilience, earns standing in your sector, and turns a compliance cost into a position worth holding.

Powered by the Climate Access Stress Test

CAST is our scored diagnostic. It reads where climate is already breaking access, supply chain, and workforce, across four access dimensions, physical reach, supply-chain integrity, workforce availability, and demand continuity, against the full climate-health year of heat, monsoon, air, and drought. The output: a scored vulnerability assessment, a set of climate-access metrics that lift straight into a BRSR or funder report and stand up under audit, and a prioritised set of interventions ranked by what to do first. Available inside a CSR engagement, or as a standalone diagnostic.

The Access-Resilience Map: the single image that shows where climate stress severs the patient's path to care.
