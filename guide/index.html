Why This Exists

We're not trying to keep this to ourselves

The scoring methods and diagnostic frameworks we use with clients stay ours. The context underneath them doesn't need to. If a plain definition of BRSR assurance requirements helps you make a better decision this week, that's a good use of an afternoon, whether or not you ever become a client.

Climate change didn't wait for a budget line to open. Neither should the information.
